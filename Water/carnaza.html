Carnaza Island

Carnaza Island, Daanbantayan, Cebu. Danbantayan’s gorgeous gem, one of the few remaining Visayan Island one can aptly term as “iddylic” the whole 173 hectares of it. Locals say the island is partly owned by a famous clan in Cebu and was fondly called Dodi’s resort after the patriarch’s grandson. for now its simply Carnaza Eco Park. The island is described as turtle shaped if viewed from above and bordered by the vast blue Visayan Sea on all sides. Here on the jewel of the island of Daanbantayan you can have the luxury to discover the island’s many varied attractions.
